Overview

For most large enterprises, the majority of help desk tickets are created due to a handful of issues related to endpoint configurations – missing patches, misconfigured group policy, insufficient disk space, etc. Many of these issues have required IT staff to physically visit the endpoint to diagnose and remediate the issue – until now.

Adaptiva Endpoint Health automatically checks the conﬁguration of endpoints, diagnoses any problems, and remediates any issues. And it does this at massive scale – hundreds of thousands of endpoints – without bandwidth throttling or disrupting business processes. Endpoint Health is the fastest, most scalable solution to manage endpoint health at scale.

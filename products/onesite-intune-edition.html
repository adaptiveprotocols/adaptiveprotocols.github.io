Enterprises are increasingly moving to modern device management, especially in the COVID-19 world we now live in. Microsoft Endpoint Manager is one of the leading platforms, but delivering content – patches, updates, etc. – across a globally distributed network without disrupting business processes remains difficult.

OneSite Intune Edition is the first and only third-party software delivery system for Microsoft Endpoint Manager, enabling Intune to scale to hundreds of thousands of endpoints per enterprise.

OneSite is now included in OneSite Cloud Learn More

Features

Seamless Migration to Intune

Migrate from ConfigMgr to Intune at your own speed. Adaptiva OneSite Intune Edition is the only solution that can facilitate a single software distribution client to be used for both ConfigMgr and Intune simultaneously. This capability has multiple benefits, including the elimination of DPs or Connected Cache servers and the ability of the customer to migrate at their own speed while supporting ConfigMgr and Intune concurrently.

Single Download from CDN to WAN

OneSite Intune Edition reduces multiple costly downloads from the cloud – content is downloaded once from the cloud and then automatically distributed content throughout the environment using peer-to-peer technology, dramatically reducing downloads across metered links.

Infrastructure Reduction

OneSite Intune Edition eliminates the necessity for additional infrastructure such as Distribution Points or Connected Cache servers when migrating from ConfigMgr. Connected Cache servers require ConfigMgr, so for organizations that want to move to Intune and don’t have ConfigMgr and don’t want each client to download from the cloud, OneSite Intune Edition creates an easy path to do that.

Guaranteed Content Delivery

OneSite Intune Edition utilizes various unique technologies to guarantee content delivery to endpoints, regardless of link quality. OneSite adapts to network constraints and latency and provides the Checkpoint Restart capability to automatically restart content delivery where it left off as machines enter and leave the network. OneSite’s Predictive Bandwidth Harvesting predicts when congestion is going to occur, and therefore is able to utilize 100% of available bandwidth without impacting business traffic.

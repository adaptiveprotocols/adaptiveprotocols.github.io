COVID-19 has forced many organizations to accelerate their digital transformation efforts to meet new customer needs and better manage mobile endpoints. As a result, more enterprises are moving to modern device management (MDM) and deploying Microsoft Endpoint Manager with Intune. However, delivering content (such as security patches, OSes, and applications) to endpoints quickly and securely across a globally distributed network without disrupting business processes remains difficult.

OneSite Intune Edition helps you overcome your content delivery challenges by integrating the world’s most advanced software distribution engine with Microsoft Intune. It is the first and only third-party software delivery system that enables Intune to scale to hundreds of thousands of endpoints per enterprise, allowing you to make the most out of your investment.

Total visibility and control over all endpoints, regardless of where they are

Introducing OneSite Cloud, the newer version of OneSite specifically created for the era of remote work.

Securely manage all your endpoints at unprecedented speed and scale whether they are on premise, connected remotely over VPN, or on the internet without a VPN connection. Use OneSite Cloud to advance the power of Microsoft ConfigMgr, Microsoft Intune, and VMware Workspace ONE and smooth migrations to modern device management and cloud enablement.

OneSite Cloud includes the functionality of OneSite for ConfigMg, OneSite Intune Edition, and OneSite VMware Edition.

Modern Device Management at Scale

Manage endpoints at unprecedented speed and scale

The ability to distribute content reliably and securely to all endpoints is key to ensure all your devices are managed and always up to date. OneSite Intune Edition is specifically designed to work with Intune to distribute software from the cloud across an enterprise’s mobile endpoints with unprecedented speed and scale, ensuring content delivery without disrupting business traffic or requiring bandwidth throttling.

Gain Complete Control and Visibility

OneSite Intune Edition automates many time-consuming activities that would otherwise require manual operations and gives IT admins complete control over the content distribution process: View all Intune and ConfigMgr content globally, control WAN transfers in real time, view / pause / resume / reprioritize and cancel content transfers, automatically detect new content and deliver it to specified locations, and customize simple and complex operations through an easy to use Workflow Designer.

OneSite Intune Edition is now part of OneSite Cloud, Adaptiva’s newest version of OneSite specifically created for the era of remote work. OneSite Cloud includes the functionality of OneSite, OneSite Intune Edition, and OneSite VMware Edition

Reduce Download and Infrastructure Costs

Experience dramatically lower costs with minimal infrastructure required. OneSite Intune Edition reduces multiple costly downloads from the cloud. Content is downloaded once from the cloud and then automatically distributed throughout the environment using peer-to-peer technology, dramatically reducing downloads across metered links.

Accelerate digital transformation

OneSite Intune Edition is the only solution that can facilitate a single software distribution client to be used for both ConfigMgr and Intune simultaneously. Breakthrough technologies in OneSite Cloud support multiple system management platforms, which helps you transition from traditional on premises infrastructure to the cloud and modern device management at your own speed to make your enterprise more agile, productive, and secure.

How OneSite Intune Edition enables faster more efficient content delivery

OneSite Intune Edition helps you make the most of your Intune investment. Adaptiva's enterprise class P2P technology provides fast and reliable content delivery with just a single download from the cloud for lower downloads and infrastructure costs, and automatically adjusts content delivery speed to ensure business traffic never gets impacted.

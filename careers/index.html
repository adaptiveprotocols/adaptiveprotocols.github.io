Join us on our Next Adventure

We started with a simple idea: enterprise IT needs a simpler, faster, and smarter way to scale. One that doesn’t require purchasing a massive server infrastructure or negatively impacting the network.

With that mission in mind, we created the world’s first smart scaling, network adaptive peer-to-peer technology. It revolutionized the systems management space and enabled companies to manage and secure endpoints across their enterprise faster and more cost effectively than anyone thought was possible.

Living our Core Values

Adaptiva is a results-driven company that invests in hard-working, genuine, talented, and creative employees who don’t settle for anything short of their best work. Much of our success can be attributed to adherence to our four core values:

Integrity

Working at Adaptiva means absolute honesty and transparency in the way we do business, with coworkers, customers, and partners.

Work Ethic

Adaptiva promotes a self-driven work environment, and our staff achieves results without being micro-managed.

Excellence

We believe in going the extra mile to deliver our best work in everything we do, every time.

Dignity of Labor

We never ask anyone to do something we wouldn’t do ourselves. Being an executive doesn’t mean you can’t do the dishes.
